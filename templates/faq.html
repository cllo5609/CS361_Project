Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ski Colorado?
Ski colorado is your "one-stop shop" for quick information about all ski areas in Colorado.
Can I get information about Resorts outside of Colorado?
Ski Colorado features only Colorado ski areas, but we are constantly updating our site so check back soon!
What is My Ski?
My Ski is a way for you to keep track of your favorite ski resorts by giving each resort you visit a rating and a comment!
Do I need to create an account?
No, Ski Colorado can be used without a user account.
Does Ski Colorado provide directions to ski areas?
Yes, once you choose the resort you would like to vist, ski colorado provides the ability to find directions to that resort!
What if I don't see the ski area that I am looking for?
Ski Colorado includes all main ski areas in Colorado. If you didn't find the area you were looking for, fell free to send us an email and let us know!

Didn't find what you were looking for or would like to make a suggestion?
